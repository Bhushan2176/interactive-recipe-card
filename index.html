Chocolate Cake

    Dry Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cup (220g) All-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup (65g) Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups (400g) Sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp Baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp Baking soda
  • ½ tsp Salt

    • Wet Ingredients

  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) Milk
  • ½ cup (120ml) Vegetable oil / melted butter
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) Hot water / hot coffee

    • For Frosting

  • 200g Dark chocolate
  • 200ml Fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • (Optional: Powdered sugar for buttercream)
  1. Dry mix: Combine flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Wet mix: Whisk together eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla.
  3. Combine: Mix the wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Add liquid: Finally, add hot water/coffee and stir until smooth.
  5. Bake: Pour into a greased tin and bake at 180°C for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool: Let the cake cool completely.
  7. Frost: Apply ganache/buttercream and decorate (berries, choco chips, etc.). ✅
  8. Serve: Slice and enjoy your delicious chocolate cake! 🍰
Preparation Time: 1 Hr 20 minutes | Serves: 10
